Features and Changes Added to Emerald Legacy

  1. Added Following Pokémon feature from Emerald Legacy Enhanced
  2. Added additional difficulty option "Limited Healing Mode" – Limits Pokécenter access and item storage. Requires visiting 3 new centers before re-healing. After Gym 6, Fly reduces the burden. You will also lose half of non-Key Items on whiteout.
  3. BUFFS – All enemy trainers now have significantly improved teams and AI. Gym Leaders, Team Aqua/Magma leadership, and the Elite Four are true boss-level threats.
  4. X-Items can be used as held items, auto-activating upon entering battle.
  5. Shiny odds slightly increased (approx. 1 in 3 playthroughs sees a shiny).
  6. Battle Frontier now sells type-boosting items for 64 BP; rare candies for 8 BP.
  7. Dynamic NE-region weather system added.
  8. A tough easter egg fight has been added to Route 119.
  9. Slight stat buffs applied to Nosepass, Tangrowth, Magneton, Spinda, Electabuzz.
  10. Ocean route encounter diversity increased. Rare water Pokémon now appear at low frequency with thought given to locations where the pokemon would naturally inhabit.
